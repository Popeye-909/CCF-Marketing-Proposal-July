# A Note on Budget Discipline & Price Resilience

The previous term was funded at a valuation of **$0.0000009 per QUBIC.** Over the course of the term, the QUBIC price declined sharply to roughly **$0.00000042** — a drop of more than **50%.**

Despite this, **all essential operations continued without interruption.** Some roles were trimmed and spending was tightened, but the core function — keeping marketing and communications consistent regardless of market conditions — was fully preserved. Paid visibility, content output, AMAs, and ecosystem coordination all continued on schedule.

This proposal reduces the marketing budget from the previous term's run-rate of approximately **$29,400/month** to **$22,400** for July — a reduction of roughly **24%.** The intent is to deliver the same operational reliability on a leaner, more price-conscious footprint.
